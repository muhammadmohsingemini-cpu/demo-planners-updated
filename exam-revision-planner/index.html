    About & Privacy

    Revision & Exam Planner is not a grading tool — there are no scores, ranks, or comparisons. It exists to make your own consistency visible so you can trust that you're on track. All data stays in this browser's local storage; nothing is ever transmitted anywhere. Clearing your browser data or switching devices/browsers will reset the app, so export a backup periodically if you'd like to keep a copy.
