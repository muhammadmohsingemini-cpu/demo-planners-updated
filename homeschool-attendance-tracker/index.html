About & Privacy

Homeschool Attendance Tracker keeps a defensible record of school days — present, late, absent, or holiday — plus the reasons behind any absences. All data stays in this browser's local storage; nothing is ever transmitted anywhere, including any evidence files you attach. Clearing your browser data or switching devices/browsers will reset the app, so export a backup periodically if you'd like to keep a copy.
