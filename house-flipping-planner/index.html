      school The 70% Rule, explained

      Pay no more than 70% of the ARV minus rehab costs. It bakes in your profit margin and a safety cushion before you ever make an offer. Use 65% in risky markets, 75% in hot ones.

      visibility Hidden costs to remember

      Utilities during the hold, insurance, property taxes, HOA dues, staging, re-listing fees, and loan points all eat margin. The misc/contingency field is there for a reason.
