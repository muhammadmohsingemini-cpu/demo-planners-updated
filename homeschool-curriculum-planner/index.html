    About & Privacy

    Homeschool Curriculum Planner replaces manual tallying with automatic, always-current progress metrics — subjects planned, topics completed, curriculum coverage, and weekly teaching hours are all calculated from your own data, never entered by hand. All data stays in this browser's local storage; nothing is ever transmitted anywhere. Clearing your browser data or switching devices/browsers will reset the app, so export a backup periodically if you'd like to keep a copy.
